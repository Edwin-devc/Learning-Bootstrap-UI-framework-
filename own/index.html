About Standard College Ntungamo

Standard College School Ntungamo is a private mixed boarding in Ntungamo district, Western Uganda. We are privately sponsored secondary with both Ordinary and Advanced levels. Standard is located in Ntungamo Municipality along Mbarara-Kabale Road.

Our core values
  • Academic excellence
  • Discipline
  • God fearing

We are registered with the Ministry of Education and Sports (MoEs) with a UNEB centre No. U1828.
We do have both academic and co-curricular activities for our students.
